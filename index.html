About Me

I have spent most my life pursuing vocational ministry. Though I will always love my vocational work, economically the positions I were working could not meet basic economic needs. I became a jack of all trades to subsidize my income while continuing to work with my ministry. Physical wear and tear on my body, provoked me to look into other avenues of employment. In my search, I began to notice a huge need for technology jobs in Nashville. After doing some research, I applied to Nashville Software School. Since I had no technical experience, they recommended me to take a six week Jump Start Class. I signed up and in May 2019 and I wrote my first line of code. I was intrigued, fascinated, and knew I wanted to learn more. I like that development allows me to continuously learn and be challenged. The more I dive into development excited I am to begin my career as a Software Developer.
